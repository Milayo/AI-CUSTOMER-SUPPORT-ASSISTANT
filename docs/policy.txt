HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?


A: 1. You can log in to your account on the website and find the corresponding order you purchased. (Note: Tracking numbers can only be viewed on a computer.) Once you have the tracking number, use this website (https://www.17track.net/en) to track your package. All order logistics information is fully trackable.

2. Check your Google Mail account for an email notification regarding "Payment Successful/Delivery" and other operations. Check the tracking number in the email and use this website (https://www.17track.net/en) to track your package. All order logistics information is fully trackable.

3. Contact our customer service and provide your order number or the email address you used to place the order. Customer service will typically respond within 24 hours.


TAX AND DUTIES

Taxes and duties are calculated according to your shipping destination and itemized on the Checkout page. Make sure you select your location to view the correct local market pricing, delivery times and shipping costs.


EU Countries:

For all orders shipped to European Union (EU) countries and Canada, we use DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) terms. This means that the prices you see on our website already include any applicable import duties or taxes due in your country. No additional duties or taxes will be charged on delivery.

By including the duty paid, we will clear customs on your behalf and cover the cost of any import taxes in your EU country. The price you see is the full amount you will have to pay.


Non-EU Countries:

We use DDU (Delivered Duty Unpaid) terms for orders shipped to countries outside the EU. Under DDU terms, the prices of products shown on our website do not include any local and import taxes at destination.

It is your responsibility as the customer to pay any applicable import duties, taxes, customs fees or clearance charges directly to the local customs office prior to receipt of the goods. The freight courier or postal service will assist in collecting taxes and duties as required.

In non-EU countries, we will provide all appropriate documentation required for accurate customs clearance. Customs policies vary widely, so if you have any questions about taxes and fees in your country, please check with your local customs office.


Once your order arrives at its destination, you will be required to pay all import duties, customs and local sales taxes for the location that you’re shipping to. After you have placed your order, we’ll email you to seek your acceptance of these terms. For an estimate of taxes and duties on your purchase, please contact your local customs.
